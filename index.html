About

Our vision is to empower individuals with a deep understanding of Apple's software evolution by providing a comprehensive and accessible OS Version History — highlighting the continuous innovation, dedication, and tireless community efforts in shaping and transforming user experiences.


Our mission is to chronologically document and preserve the rich history of Apple's operating systems, fostering a deeper appreciation for the company's relentless pursuit of perfection. Through this, we aim to enable users, developers, and enthusiasts to recognize, recall, and reflect on the pivotal milestones and development phases that have shaped the modern digital landscape.
